The Dark Knight (2008) is a superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan, starring Christian Bale as Batman and Heath Ledger as the Joker. The movie explores the battle between Batman and the Joker, who wreaks havoc on Gotham, testing Batman's limits and morality in a gripping narrative of chaos and order.

  • Director: Christopher Nolan.
  • Release Year: 2008
  • Genre: Superhero, crime thriller.
